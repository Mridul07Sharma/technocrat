About Us

What is ES-Crew?

The idea behind the ES-Crew is to Construct a website that connect service providers with service seekers. If you are searching for a laborer, a plumber, a mechanic etc, our website will be studded with hailing professionals in all. The website will provide a login portal for both the service seekers and service providers. There are many unemployed educated youth those who have the skills but are still jobless. They can register themselves on the website providing their specialization, area(locality), etc and similarly the service seekers will login specify their requirement. The service of our website shall be across India serving all pincodes and areas.

How ES-Crew Works?

Now I will explain you with help of an example - Suppose A shopkeeper has 2 of his staff members on holiday and he requires 2 workers for one day then he can register himself as a a service seeker on our website and from our website he can hire two laborer for a day. He will be showed a list of workers registered on our website so he can hire anyone from the list. Now I will explain you with an example of service provider, service provider will register on our portal and will be hired when somebody requires. Our model is different from urban company because it will provide job to all the skilled workers and our services shall be available in all the areas.

Why ES-Crew?

Service Provider

People sitting in small and unreached localities of the world sometimes finds it so hard to look for a plumber,mechanic,workers for some bussiness,etc .So,our main motive is to help such people with all those services they are looking for.

Employment Opportunities

Many young minds especially in India who are having skills are still unemployed because of the less connectivity between job seeker and job provider.Our website is built with the motive to ensure that connectivity and provide employment opportunities to such individuals.

Budget Friendly

It happens many times that whenever you are in a urgent need of a service then those service providers charges you a lot seeing the urgency of your need .So, our website will show you all the details of the service provider along with their charges ,so that you can choose acoording to your budget.
